The Boring Duck Story

Once there was a boring duck named Stan. Stan lives in a pond near a really tall tree with all his friends, Squirrel, Grasshopper, and Fox. Stan was hungry so he decided to ask his friends what he would like to eat.

Squirrel thought Stan should eat a burger and fries. Fox thought that he should eat something sweet and suggested ice cream.

Ice cream sounded great to Stan, so he decided to go out and find an ice cream shop. Once he did he was so excited for the ice cream he bought the biggest sundae they offered.

Once Stan got home he was so sick from all the ice cream that he went straight to bed. But when he woke up in the morning he was ready for more ice cream. THE END
